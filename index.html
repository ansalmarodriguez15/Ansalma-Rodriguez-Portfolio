About Me

Hi! I'm Ansalma, a senior mechanical engineering student at The University of Texas at Austin with a passion for continuous improvement and problem solving.

Through internships at companies like Micron Technology, GE Appliances, and Southwest Research Institute, I've gained hands-on experience in equipment engineering, product quality, and electromechanical design. Additionally, I have 1 year in research experience in additive manufacturing and semiconductor packaging.

As an active member of the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE), I've developed leadership skills through community engagement and professional development.

I'm passionate about increasing diversity in STEM fields and plan to pursue a Master's in Robotics. My technical skills span from programming, data analysis, and hands-on prototyping. I'm always eager to get my hands dirty and learn the depths of new technologies. Overall, I aim to be a well-rounded and highly educated engineer!
